About Company

YISO Solution & System is sofware and system support company.

Three buddies who were both practical mathematicians and dreamers at heart founded it in 2007. They wanted to contribute to society by utilising their love of science and software.

With the creation of software for horizontal drilling, Yiso made its debut on the EU software market. It calculates the drilling route using mathematical modelling, and it continuously regulates the drilling operation.

We are still key partners today, 13 years and hundreds of successful releases later.In parallel, Yiso forged a collaboration with Lausanne University in Switzerland. Then came more difficult but rewarding projects, such the aeroplane self-testing system and the compression behaviour modelling of solid foam.
